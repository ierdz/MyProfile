Project Idea

Overview

The project will be working towards developing an application for apple/android/windows users to get in touch and up to date with the latest trends. Whether it is fashion or entertainment, this app will have users be in the know of what’s happening interstate and globally.

Motivation

Seeing as more and more people rely on social media sites for news, the project will be aimed at users to not use social media sites to find out news and trends of either a particular game, shoe, clothing brand and artist. The aim of this application is to increase awareness and for users to gain interest on their preferred news genre, without having to use social media sites as they tend to get boring overtime

Description

This app would be divided into categories, catering to all users. What’s featured in the app would be the latest news in games, music, clothing and random. These categories are maintained by the team which scout the internet or real-life to uncover information on any new news or leaks. An interesting feature in the app would be the ‘random’ news generator. When in the app, users have the option to select the random button to be entertained with whichever news from any category.

Features

Music

As music is constantly growing daily, the genres we are mainly focussing on are: Rap/Hip-Hop, Pop, Techno, House and Alternative. These particular genres have had a strong influence in Generations Y and Z. With this being said, respectively they would have a sense of interest in receiving updates within these genres.

Gaming

Gaming, as well as streaming have been increasingly becoming recognised towards consumers over the years. With ‘Battle Royale’ games such as; Fortnite, consumers would like to be in the know of new game releases and or news on professional gamers and eSports tournaments.

Clothing

High-fashion, streetwear and the shoe-game are on a whole new level now that Kanye West’s ‘Yeezy’ and Virgil Abloh’s ‘Off-White’ collection are taking control of consumers how the shoes are showcased. With limited availability on certain shoes, this app provides any leaks and raffle guides for all users to have the chance to purchase shoes on time.
